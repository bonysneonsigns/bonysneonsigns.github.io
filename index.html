About Us

Since 2003, Bony's Neon & Signs has been a full service custom neon sign company in San Jose. We treat every client with the same amount of dedication and respect, and each project with careful design consideration and attention to detail. Whether you need a simple neon sign to hang on your window, or a creative sign that illustrates your brand's vision and personality, we can execute your project from design to installation.
